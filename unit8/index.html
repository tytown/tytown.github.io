ContraTravel.

by Thomas McCoy

...NOW AVAILABLE

ContraTravel is the award-winning true story of one man's life experiencing the world from a wildly different perspective. The author demonstrates how to truly enjoy the wonders of this planet - in your own unique way.

Experience the ride of living against the norm:

  • Running in the fast, cut-throat field of international banking and trade;
  • Plotting the rescue and recovery of a Hong Kong billionare's investment;
  • Opening and directing a major US State Government office in Japan, reporting directly to a U.S. Governor and former U.S. Attorney General;
  • Encouters with a snake huge enough to swallow a small tribe, while swimming in a Belizean jungle river;
  • Teaching International Economics at the Graduate School of a Major Midwestern University;
  • Running for Congress against the megalithic Bush family political jugnaut;

... and so much more.
